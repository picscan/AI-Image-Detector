Privacy Policy

Welcome to Picscan Online ("Picscan", "we", "our", or "us"). Picscan Online provides tools to help internet users, creators, journalists, and the general public evaluate whether an image is authentic or generated by Artificial Intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit or use our services at https://picscan.online.

1. Summary

We respect your privacy. In short:

  • Images you upload for detection are processed in a privacy-first manner and are not stored by default (see Image Handling below).
  • We use cookies and third-party services (including Google AdSense) to deliver and measure ads and to improve site functionality.
  • You have rights regarding your personal data under applicable laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA). See Your rights.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information you provide

  • Optional contact information (for example if you send us an email or submit feedback).
  • Support requests or other messages you send directly to us.

b. Images and analysis data

When you upload an image to the detection tool, Picscan analyzes the image to provide a result. By default, Picscan is designed with a privacy-first approach:

  • Images are processed for analysis and are not retained on our servers after processing unless you explicitly choose to save or share results (we will always make that option clear).
  • We may temporarily cache data to complete the analysis request, but such transient data is deleted promptly and not used for any unrelated purpose.

c. Automatically collected data

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, such as:

  • IP address (may be used in anonymized form for analytics and abuse prevention).
  • Browser type and version, operating system, and device information.
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, referral source, and interactions with the site.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to operate the site and provide analytics. Cookies may be set by us or by third-party services we use (for example, Google services used for analytics and advertising).

Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to serve ads on Picscan. Google may use cookies and other identifiers to deliver and measure ads and to personalize advertising based on your interests. For information about how Google uses data and to manage ad personalization, see Google Ads Settings.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the detection service and its features.
  • To process and analyze images you submit for the purpose of returning detection results.
  • To prevent abuse, detect fraud, and ensure the security of our platform.
  • To respond to inquiries, support requests, and communications from users.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To display relevant advertising and measure ad performance (including using third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense).

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may share data with trusted vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, email). These providers are contractually required to protect your data.
  • Legal reasons: If required by law, to respond to legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Picscan, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction (we will require the acquirer to honor this Privacy Policy).

7. Data Retention

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, limit processing, or export your data. Examples:
