About Picscan Online

Picscan AI is a trusted digital platform created to help internet users, content creators, journalists, and everyday people analyze whether an image is authentic or generated by Artificial Intelligence. As AI-generated visuals become increasingly realistic, verifying what’s genuine has never been more important.

Our platform uses modern detection techniques to highlight visual patterns that may indicate AI involvement. The process is simple, fast, and designed with privacy in mind — no personal data or uploaded images are permanently stored. Picscan’s goal is to make visual verification accessible to everyone, anywhere in the world.

At Picscan AI, we believe that technology should promote honesty, not confusion. We aim to raise public awareness about digital authenticity while supporting ethical and responsible AI usage. By empowering users to verify what they see online, we help build a safer and more transparent digital environment.

Why Choose Picscan AI?

  • Accurate and easy-to-use photo analysis powered by AI research.
  • Privacy first approach, uploaded images are never stored or shared.
  • Ad-friendly and mobile-responsive design that complies with Google AdSense policies.
  • Optimized for accessibility and multilingual support for global users.
  • Constantly updated with improvements for higher detection accuracy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote trust, transparency, and critical awareness in the digital era. Whether you’re checking a viral photo, verifying research material, or simply exploring AI capabilities, Picscan AI provides a clear, reliable way to evaluate image authenticity with confidence.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to continuous development and transparent communication. Every feature we release is carefully reviewed to maintain accuracy, security, and ethical standards. Picscan AI collaborates with independent experts and the broader tech community to ensure our service remains credible, educational, and beneficial for users worldwide.
