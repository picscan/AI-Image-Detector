Frequently Asked Questions

How does this tool detect AI-generated images?

The system analyzes texture, lighting, reflections, and other small details that AI often fails to replicate naturally.

Can this tool guarantee 100% accuracy?

No AI detector is perfect. This tool provides the most probable assessment based on advanced visual analysis, but cannot guarantee absolute certainty.

What file formats are supported?

You can upload JPG, PNG, or WEBP formats. Large or uncommon files may take longer to process.

Is my uploaded image stored anywhere?

No. All images are analyzed temporarily in memory and not stored after processing.

Does it work for AI-generated artwork or illustrations?

Yes, the tool can also detect AI-created digital art, though accuracy may vary for stylized images.

Can I use this for commercial verification?

You can use the results for assistance in verification, but always cross-check for legal or commercial use.

What happens if the tool says “Uncertain”?

It means the system found both real and AI-like characteristics, making it unable to decide confidently.

Does lighting or compression affect detection accuracy?

Yes, poor lighting or high compression can reduce analysis precision.

Can I test photos found online?

Yes, you can upload any public photo for authenticity checking.

Will this tool improve over time?

Yes, the AI detector is continuously updated with new data to improve accuracy and reliability.

Picscan Labs provides AI-powered tools to help users verify whether an image is real or generated by AI technology.
